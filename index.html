Coca-Cola Brand

The Coca-Cola Company stands as an iconic beacon in the world of beverages, renowned for its rich history, global presence, and enduring popularity. Since its inception in 1886, when pharmacist John Pemberton concocted the original formula in Atlanta, Georgia, this effervescent drink has become a symbol of refreshment and happiness worldwide. Its secret recipe, guarded closely and known to only a select few, has evolved into an unparalleled array of flavors and variations, satisfying diverse palates across more than 200 countries.
